How the AI works
The AI uses Minimax to recursively explore all future game states, assuming both players play optimally. Alpha-Beta pruning eliminates branches that cannot affect the outcome, cutting nodes evaluated by up to 90%. A heuristic scoring function evaluates non-terminal positions based on center control, connected pieces, and opponent threats. Difficulty controls look-ahead depth: Easy = 2, Medium = 5, Hard = 8.
